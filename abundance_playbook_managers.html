Your Role & KPIs
What you own as an outlet manager and how your performance is measured.

  • Upselling execution — drinks and dessert attachment

What success looks like
A great outlet manager runs the outlet so well that the owner and ops manager rarely need to intervene. You spot problems before they escalate, you develop your team, and your numbers speak for themselves.
Monday check-in (weekly)
Every Monday morning, send your ops manager a 3-line update:
  • One number — last week's key metric (sales, drinks %, dessert %)
  • One win — something that went well
  • One blocker — something you need help with

Revenue
Sales & Revenue
How to track, reconcile, and improve sales performance at your outlet.
Daily sales entry
  • Key in daily sales into the Ops Dashboard every day
  • Cross-check POS backend vs keyed-in sales — flag discrepancies immediately
  • Delivery sales reconciliation: match platform payouts to actual orders
  • Delivery disputes: track and submit within 48 hours of incident
Upselling — your biggest lever
Upselling is not left to chance at Abundance. It is built into our service SOP. Your job is to make sure every table is offered a drink and every transaction is offered a dessert — consistently, every shift.
Drinks target≥80% of customers order 1 drink
Dessert target≥75% of transactions include 1 dessert
Refer to the Upselling SOP for exact scripts and timing.
Competitor monitoring
  • Update the Competitor Monitoring sheet when not busy during off-peak hours
  • Record promotions, pricing changes, and crowd levels at nearby competitors
  • Flag anything unusual or interesting to your ops manager
Inventory
Stock & Ordering
How to manage stock at your outlet — ordering, stocktake, and variance reporting.
Ordering principles
  • Order based on par levels — never by gut feel
  • Refer to the Ordering Groupchat Purchase Lists for par levels and MOQ per supplier
  • Never over-order — check current stock before placing orders
  • All ordering goes through the designated supplier groupchats
  • Supplier invoice verification is handled at outlet level — check invoices on delivery
Month end stock take (submit by 5th)
  • Count all stock at end of month — every item, every category
  • Enter counts into Month End Stock Take Form
  • Variance between opening stock + purchases − closing stock = usage
  • If variance is unexplained, flag it with a note before submitting
  • Submit to ops manager by the 5th of the following month
Stock movement form (submit by 5th)
  • Record all purchases, transfers in/out, and closing balances
  • Helps identify if your outlet is over-ordering or under-ordering
  • Submit alongside stock take form by the 5th
Wastage is money leaving the business. If you notice recurring wastage on a specific item, flag it to the CK Manager — it may be a par level or portioning issue.
People
Staff Management
Scheduling, leave, payroll, training, and team development.
Monthly schedule (submit by 15th)
  • Plan next month's roster and submit by the 15th of current month
  • Ensure minimum staffing coverage for all shifts, especially weekends
  • Coordinate part-time availability before finalising the schedule
  • Use the Part-time Rate Legend to verify pay rates before scheduling
Leave management
  • All leave requests go into the Leave Block Out sheet
  • Inform ops manager of any update to leave requests
  • Do not approve leave that leaves the outlet understaffed without a replacement plan
Part-time payroll (submit last day of month)
  • Fill in your outlet's Part-time Payroll Form by the last day of each month
  • Cross-check against the Part-time Rate Legend for correct rates
  • Submit to Finance Admin for processing
RH → RH Part-Time Form · JB → JB Part-Time Form · CS → CS Part-Time Form
Product training
  • Every new staff member must complete the Abundance Training onboarding within 2 months
  • Run food tastings using the Food Product Knowledge deck (EN or Chinese)
  • Track each staff member's progress in the Product Knowledge Tracker
  • You are responsible for ensuring your team knows the menu well enough to upsell

Standards
Audit & Compliance
Monthly outlet audits, discount compliance, and service standards.
Monthly outlet audit
The outlet audit covers cleanliness, service standards, product knowledge, and SOP compliance. Target pass rate is ≥90%.
  • Audit is conducted monthly and submitted to the ops manager
  • Use the Outlet Audit Marking form
  • Areas scored: cleanliness, service, product knowledge, upselling execution, SOP compliance
  • Any scoring below 90% requires an improvement plan submitted within 7 days
Discount compliance
  • Only apply discounts listed in the Discounts Summary — no exceptions
  • No double discounts — one discount per transaction unless explicitly stated
  • Ensure minimum spend requirements are met before applying any discount
  • Misuse of discounts is a serious compliance breach — flag any suspicious cases
Petty cash (submit by 25th)
  • Record all petty cash expenditure with receipts
  • Submit to Finance Admin (YX) by the 25th of each month
  • No personal expenses — petty cash is for outlet operational needs only
Food return guidelines
  • Follow the Food Return Guidelines document for all food lapse situations
  • Do not promise replacements or refunds without following the guidelines
  • Escalate all serious food complaints to the ops manager
  • Document all incidents — even if resolved on the spot

Delivery Disputes
All order cancellations by delivery platforms must be disputed. Use these scripts.
Rule: ALL order cancellations by delivery platforms must be disputed by submitting a dispute report — unless there is clear photo evidence that the cancellation was our fault (e.g. foreign objects in food).
CCTV access for evidence: RH — contact Danny · JB — contact Owner (YX) · CS — contact Owner (YX)
Dispute scripts — tap to expand
1. Missing Item +
Before CCTV / without evidence:
"Please provide evidence of missing items."
With evidence:
"I would like to seek a refund for the following order. We have checked the packing process, and the items were prepared and packed correctly before we handed to the rider. Kindly assist to provide refund to this case." *Attach evidence*
2. Wrong Item Sent +
"Please provide evidence of wrong items."
With evidence:
"I would like to seek a refund for the following order. Based on our check, the dish was prepared according to the order reflected in the system before dispatch. The order was packed and handed to the rider accordingly. Kindly assist to provide a refund to this case." *Attach evidence*
3. Missing Sauce / Condiments +
"I would like to seek a refund for the following order. Sauces are packed together but in a separate container as part of our packing checklist before the order is handed to the rider. We have checked with the team and the order was packed accordingly before dispatch. Kindly assist to review this case." *Attach evidence*
4. Missing Cutlery / Napkins +
"I would like to seek a refund for the following order. Please note that the complete food order was handed to the rider. Cutlery and napkins are strictly optional, complimentary accessories and are never considered part of the paid menu items. It is disproportionate and unfair for the merchant to bear the cost of a full refund for a completed food order over complimentary accessories. We kindly request that you review this case and reverse the refund charge to our account."
5. Spilled / Leaking Items +
"I would like to seek a refund for the following order. The order was packed and sealed before being handed to the rider and the packaging was intact at dispatch. The spillage may have occurred during the delivery process. Kindly assist to provide a refund in this case."
6. Foreign Object in Food +
"Please provide evidence of the foreign object."
With evidence:
"I would like to seek a refund for the following order. We have checked with the kitchen team and reviewed the preparation process. No irregularities were observed during preparation or packing before dispatch. Kindly assist to review this case."
7. Food Quality Complaint +
"I would like to seek refund for the following order. The dish was freshly prepared according to our standard recipe and dispatched shortly after cooking. As food quality can be affected by delivery time and handling during transit, we would appreciate your assistance to review this case."
8. Late Delivery / Food Cold +
"I would like to seek a refund for the following order. The order was prepared within our usual preparation time and handed to the rider shortly after completion. Delivery timing depends on rider allocation and route. Kindly assist to review this case."
9. Customer Ordered Wrongly +
"I would like to seek refund for the following order. We have reviewed the order details and the items prepared match the order submitted through the platform. The order was packed and dispatched accordingly. Kindly assist to review this case."
